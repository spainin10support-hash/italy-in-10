# Amalfi Coast — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Naples Capodichino (NAP)
- **Bus**: Alibus €5 to Napoli Centrale (20 min) + Circumvesuviana €4.60 to Sorrento (70 min) + SITA bus €2.40 to Positano (50-60 min). Total ~€12, 3-4.5h. Also Pintour direct airport→Amalfi €20, 2h10.
- **Train**: Same as bus route. Total ~€12-15, 3-4.5h.
- **Ferry**: Alibus €5 + metro/taxi to Molo Beverello + ferry to Positano/Amalfi €20-25 (1.5h). Total ~€27-32, 2.5-3.5h. Seasonal Apr-Oct.
- **Taxi**: Official taxi to Positano €150-170, to Amalfi €170-190. 1.5-2h. Pre-booked private transfer from €130.

### Salerno Costa d'Amalfi (QSR)
- **Bus**: Airlink bus €6 to Salerno station (40 min) then SITA bus along coast. Total ~€9-11, 1.5-2.5h.
- **Train**: Free shuttle to Pontecagnano station + train to Salerno ~€2 (10 min). Then bus/ferry to coast.
- **Ferry**: Taxi/transfer to Salerno port (€35-70, 20 min) + ferry to Amalfi €9 (35 min) or Positano ~€12 (50 min). Seasonal Apr-Oct.
- **Taxi**: Official taxi: Amalfi €110-160, Positano €140-190. Pre-booked: Amalfi €110-170, Positano €130-180.

### Tips
NAP has far more flight options year-round. QSR is closer (45 km vs 75 km) but limited seasonal routes. Book transfers in advance Jul-Aug. Cheapest NAP→Positano: Circumvesuviana+SITA bus ~€12.

## Day Trips

### Capri
- **What**: Blue Grotto, Monte Solaro chairlift, Faraglioni, Gardens of Augustus
- **Transport**: Ferry from Positano €23 (30 min) or Amalfi €25 (60 min). Services Apr-Oct.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Pompeii
- **What**: Roman city frozen in 79 AD — preserved streets, frescoes, casts
- **Transport**: Circumvesuviana from Sorrento €3.30 (35 min). Entry €20.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Naples
- **What**: Archaeology museum, Spaccanapoli, pizza birthplace, Castel dell'Ovo
- **Transport**: Train from Salerno €5 (40-60 min) or Sorrento €4.60 (70 min). Ferry from Sorrento €15 (40 min).
- **Best for**: Culture

### Ravello
- **What**: Villa Rufolo, Villa Cimbrone Terrace of Infinity, Ravello Festival concerts
- **Transport**: SITA bus from Amalfi €1.50 (25 min, every 30 min). No ferry access.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Sorrento
- **What**: Limoncello tastings, cliffside views, Bay of Naples panorama
- **Transport**: Ferry from Positano €16 (25-55 min). SITA bus from Positano €2.40 (50 min).
- **Best for**: Family
