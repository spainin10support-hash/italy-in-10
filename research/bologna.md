# Bologna — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Bologna Guglielmo Marconi (BLQ)
- **Bus**: Bus 944 €4, 20-25 min; Marconi Express 949 €9.80, 25-30 min to Piazza Malpighi
- **Train**: Marconi Express monorail €12.80 one-way (€23.30 return), 7 min to Bologna Centrale, runs 5:40am-midnight every 7 min peak
- **Taxi**: €20-30, 15-25 min to city centre

### Tips
Cheapest is Bus 944 (€4). Marconi Express fastest (7 min). Contactless Pay&Go at train gates. After midnight, Line Q runs.

## Day Trips

### Modena
- **What**: Romanesque Cathedral (UNESCO), Piazza Grande, Ferrari/Maserati/Lamborghini, balsamic vinegar
- **Transport**: Train from Bologna Centrale 20-35 min, €3.85-€16, every ~20-30 min
- **Best for**: Culture, food lovers, couples

### Parma
- **What**: Parmesan cheese, Prosciutto di Parma, Correggio frescoes, Teatro Farnese
- **Transport**: Train ~50-70 min, €7.35-€21.50. Frequent departures.
- **Best for**: Food lovers, culture, couples

### Ravenna
- **What**: Byzantine mosaics — Basilica di San Vitale, Galla Placidia, Sant'Apollinare Nuovo
- **Transport**: Train ~1h direct, €9-€12. 19-26 direct trains daily.
- **Best for**: Culture, art lovers, families

### Ferrara
- **What**: Estense Castle, Palazzo dei Diamanti, bike-friendly, cappellacci di zucca
- **Transport**: Train 30-50 min, €4.75-€10, every ~30 min
- **Best for**: Culture, families, couples

### San Marino
- **What**: Oldest republic, three medieval fortresses on Mount Titano, duty-free shopping
- **Transport**: Train Bologna→Rimini (~1h, €10-15) + Bonelli Bus to San Marino (€6, 45 min)
- **Best for**: Culture, couples, history buffs
