# Lake Como — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Milan Malpensa (MXP)
- **Bus**: Flibco direct to Como, 50 min, ~€10
- **Train**: Malpensa Exp to Saronno + regional to Como Nord Lago, 60 min, ~€12
- **Taxi**: 55-70 min, ~€120-160

### Milan Linate (LIN)
- **Bus** + train: ~1.5h, ~€10-13
- **Taxi**: ~€95-140

### Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY)
- **Bus** + train: ~2h, ~€13-15
- **Taxi**: ~€129-165

### Tips
MXP is most practical. Flibco bus is cheapest direct option. Book online.

## Day Trips

### Bellagio
- **What**: Pearl of the Lake at Como Y-fork. Villa Melzi gardens (€8), cobbled lanes, designer boutiques
- **Transport**: Ferry from Como (2h slow/45min fast, €12.40/€18.80). Central lake day pass €15.20.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Varenna
- **What**: Pastel fishing village, Passeggiata degli Innamorati, Villa Monastero gardens (€10)
- **Transport**: Direct train from Milan Centrale (1h, €7.40). Ferry from Bellagio (15 min, €4.60).
- **Best for**: Couples

### Lugano (Switzerland)
- **What**: Lake Lugano, Monte San Salvatore funicular, Swiss chocolate, high-end shopping
- **Transport**: Direct train from Milan Centrale (1h 15min, ~€15). Or from Como by train (30 min).
- **Best for**: Culture

### Milan
- **What**: Duomo rooftop, The Last Supper, La Scala, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, Navigli
- **Transport**: Train from Como San Giovanni (40 min, €5.20) or Varenna (1h, €7.40). Every 30-60 min.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Bergamo
- **What**: Medieval Citta Alta, Piazza Vecchia, Venetian walls (UNESCO), funicular to San Vigilio
- **Transport**: Direct train from Milan Centrale (48 min, €6). Hourly.
- **Best for**: Culture
