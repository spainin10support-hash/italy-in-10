# Naples — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Naples Capodichino (NAP)
- **Bus**: Alibus shuttle €5 one-way, 15-35 min to city centre (Piazza Garibaldi/Molo Beverello), runs 05:30-midnight every 20-30 min
- **Train**: No direct metro. Take Alibus (€5) to Napoli Centrale then Circumvesuviana for regional destinations
- **Taxi**: Fixed fare €21-€28.50 depending on zone, 15-30 min; shared taxi option €6 pp

### Tips
Alibus €5 is best value. Taxis operate on fixed fares (confirm before boarding). Shared taxis €6/person between airport/station/port.

## Day Trips

### Pompeii
- **What**: Roman city frozen in 79 AD — forum, amphitheatre, thermal baths, plaster casts
- **Transport**: Circumvesuviana from Napoli Garibaldi to Pompei Scavi, €3.30, 35 min. Station 50m from entrance.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Herculaneum
- **What**: Smaller but better-preserved. Wooden structures, vivid mosaics, far fewer crowds
- **Transport**: Circumvesuviana to Ercolano Scavi, €2.80, 20 min. 5-min walk to entrance.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Amalfi Coast
- **What**: Positano, Amalfi, Ravello — dramatic cliffs, pastel villages, turquoise water
- **Transport**: Ferry from Molo Beverello €15-€25, 1-2 hr (Apr-Oct). Or Circumvesuviana to Sorrento €4 then SITA bus.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Capri
- **What**: Blue Grotto, Monte Solaro chairlift, Faraglioni, Villa Jovis
- **Transport**: Hydrofoil from Molo Beverello €22-€28, 50 min. Ferry €14-€19, 80 min. 20+ daily in summer.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Caserta Palace
- **What**: 18th-century palace with 1,200 rooms, Versailles-style park with waterfalls
- **Transport**: Regional train from Napoli Centrale to Caserta, €4-€5, 40 min. Station opposite palace.
- **Best for**: Family
