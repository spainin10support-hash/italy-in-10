# Florence — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Florence Peretola (FLR)
- **Bus**: No direct bus; tram T2 to city centre
- **Train**: No train; tram T2 to SMN station  
- **Taxi**: ~€22-25, 15-20 min to centre

### Pisa Galileo Galilei (PSA)
- **Bus**: Terravision/flibco bus, ~€10-15, 1h 15min, hourly
- **Train**: Trenitalia regional, ~€8-10, ~1h, every 15-30 min
- **Taxi**: ~€120-150, 1h 15min private transfer

### Tips
FLR tram T2 runs 5:00-00:30 (Fri-Sat until 02:00), every 5-18 min, €2. Pisa train is fastest value option; book regional tickets on the day.

## Day Trips

### Siena
- **What**: UNESCO medieval city with shell-shaped Piazza del Campo, Gothic Duomo, Palio horse race
- **Transport**: Train from SMN ~1.5h, €10-15; bus ~1h 15min, €7-10
- **Best for**: Culture

### Pisa
- **What**: Leaning Tower, Piazza dei Miracoli, Cathedral, Baptistery
- **Transport**: Train from SMN ~1h, €8-10, up to 47 trains/day. 20 min walk to tower.
- **Best for**: Family

### San Gimignano
- **What**: Hilltop medieval town with 14 surviving tower-houses, gelato shops, panoramic views
- **Transport**: Bus to Poggibonsi (1h, €5-8) then local bus 130 (15 min)
- **Best for**: Culture

### Chianti
- **What**: Wine region with rolling vineyards, olive groves, medieval castles, wine tasting
- **Transport**: Best by organised tour (~€49-69) or car. Bus 365 from Florence ~1h.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Fiesole
- **What**: Ancient Etruscan-Roman hill town with ruins, cathedral, panoramic Florence views
- **Transport**: Bus #7 from Piazza San Marco ~25-30 min, €1.70, every 20 min
- **Best for**: Culture
