# Cinque Terre — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Genoa Cristoforo Colombo (GOA)
- **Bus**: Volabus €10 (30 min) to Genoa stations then train (1.25-1.75h, €8-12). Total ~2-2.5h, €10-22.
- **Train**: Airlink bus €2 (5 min) to Sestri Ponente station then regional train to Monterosso/La Spezia (1.25-2h, €8-15). Total ~1.5-2.5h, €10-17.
- **Taxi**: Direct transfer. ~1.5h (105 km). €160-200 taxi, €200-370 pre-booked.

### Pisa Galileo Galilei (PSA)
- **Bus**: PisaMover €5.90-6.50 (5 min) to Pisa Centrale + train to La Spezia (1-1.5h, €8-12) + CT Express (5-15 min, €4-5). Total ~1.5-2h, €15-25.
- **Train**: Same as bus route but all rail. Total ~1.5-2h, €15-25.
- **Taxi**: Direct to Monterosso/La Spezia. ~1.5h (84 km). €150-200.

### Tips
Pisa is closer (84 km vs 112 km) and generally cheaper/easier by train. Both require CT Express from La Spezia (€4-5 per ride).

## Day Trips

### Portofino
- **What**: Pastel fishing village, Piazzetta harbour, designer boutiques, Castello Brown
- **Transport**: Train to Santa Margherita Ligure (1-1.5h, €5-7), then bus #782 (20 min, €3) or seasonal ferry (15 min, €8-10)
- **Best for**: Couples, culture

### Genoa
- **What**: Largest medieval old town in Italy, Europe's biggest aquarium, Rolli Palaces (UNESCO)
- **Transport**: Direct regional train from Monterosso/Vernazza to Genoa Piazza Principe (1.25-1.75h, €8-12). Hourly.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Lerici and Tellaro
- **What**: Gulf of Poets towns — medieval castles, colourful houses, Byron's Cave
- **Transport**: Train to La Spezia (10 min, €4), then ATC bus L/S (30 min, €2.50) to Lerici
- **Best for**: Couples, family

### Pisa
- **What**: Leaning Tower, Piazza dei Miracoli (UNESCO), Baptistery, Cathedral
- **Transport**: Train from La Spezia to Pisa Centrale (1-1.5h, €8-12). Frequent regionals.
- **Best for**: Culture, family

### Lucca
- **What**: Walled Renaissance city, bike-friendly ramparts, Roman amphitheatre, Guinigi Tower
- **Transport**: Train from La Spezia to Lucca via Viareggio/Pisa (1.25-1.75h, €8-12)
- **Best for**: Culture, family
