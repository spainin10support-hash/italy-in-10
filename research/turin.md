# Turin — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Turin Caselle (TRN)
- **Bus**: Arriva Italia €7.50 (€8.50 on board), 45-50 min to Porta Nuova/Porta Susa; Flibco from €3.99 online, 25-30 min to C.so Vittorio Emanuele II
- **Train**: SFM4/6/7 regional €3.60, 30-35 min to Porta Susa, every 30 min weekdays
- **Taxi**: €36-50 fixed fare, 30 min; €50-55 after 10pm

### Tips
Train is cheapest/fastest. Flibco bus best value at €3.99 online. Torino+Piemonte Card includes unlimited public transport.

## Day Trips

### Alba & Barolo Wine Region
- **What**: UNESCO Langhe hills, Barolo and Barbaresco vineyards, white truffle fair (autumn)
- **Transport**: Train Porta Nuova/Susa to Alba €6-11, 1h20min. Limited bus to villages. Car recommended.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Aosta Valley
- **What**: Roman ruins (Arch of Augustus, theatre), Alpine backdrop, Mont Blanc/Matterhorn gateway
- **Transport**: Train Porta Nuova to Aosta €10-15, ~2h. Hourly departures.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Sacra di San Michele
- **What**: Mountaintop abbey (inspiration for The Name of the Rose), 960m up, panoramic Susa Valley views
- **Transport**: Train+bus €6.30 to Avigliana then bus (seasonal Sat/Sun only, Apr-Oct). Taxi from Avigliana €25.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Vercelli
- **What**: Rice paddies, Basilica di Sant'Andrea (Romanesque-Gothic), Vercelli Book manuscript
- **Transport**: Train from Porta Nuova/Susa from €7.70, 35-50 min. ~25 trains daily.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Langhe Hills (UNESCO)
- **What**: Vineyards, medieval villages (Barolo, La Morra, Neive), wine tastings, cycling
- **Transport**: Train to Alba €6-11, 1h10min. Car or organised tour strongly recommended.
- **Best for**: Couples
