# Milan — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Milan Malpensa (MXP)
- **Bus**: Terravision/Malpensa Shuttle €10, 50 min to Milano Centrale, every 20-30 min
- **Train**: Malpensa Express €15, 50 min to Cadorna/Centrale, every 30 min
- **Taxi**: €95 flat rate, 45 min

### Milan Linate (LIN)
- **Bus**: Starfly/Air Bus 73 €5-7, 25 min to Milano Centrale, every 20-30 min
- **Train**: Metro M4 €2.20, 12 min to San Babila, every 5 min
- **Taxi**: €30-40 metered, 20 min

### Bergamo Orio al Serio (BGY)
- **Bus**: Orio Shuttle/Terravision/Flixbus €10-12, 50 min to Milano Centrale, every 30 min
- **Train**: No direct rail; bus to Bergamo station then train (€7-10, ~1.5h total)
- **Taxi**: €100+, 50 min

### Tips
MXP is main hub furthest out (50km). LIN is closest (7km) with fast M4 metro. BGY serves budget airlines; Orio Shuttle is best value.

## Day Trips

### Lake Como
- **What**: Alpine lake with elegant villas, Bellagio, Varenna, boat ferries
- **Transport**: Train Milano Centrale to Como S. Giovanni (35-40 min, €5) or Varenna (60 min, €8). Ferries €5-15.
- **Best for**: Couples

### Bergamo
- **What**: Medieval hilltop Città Alta, funicular, Venetian walls (UNESCO), Piazza Vecchia
- **Transport**: Train Milano Centrale to Bergamo (50 min, €5-7). Funicular to Città Alta €1.30.
- **Best for**: Culture

### Pavia
- **What**: University city with Certosa di Pavia monastery, Visconti Castle, Romanesque churches
- **Transport**: Train Milano Centrale/Rogoredo to Pavia (20-30 min, €5)
- **Best for**: Culture

### Verona
- **What**: Juliet's balcony, Roman Arena (opera venue), Piazza delle Erbe, Ponte Pietra
- **Transport**: High-speed train Milano Centrale to Verona Porta Nuova (1h 20min, €15-25)
- **Best for**: Couples

### Turin
- **What**: Egyptian Museum, Mole Antonelliana, Savoy palaces, chocolate culture
- **Transport**: High-speed train Milano Centrale to Torino Porta Nuova (1h, €15-30)
- **Best for**: Culture
