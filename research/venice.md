# Venice — Research Data
Last Updated: 2026-06-09

## Airport Transfers

### Venice Marco Polo (VCE)
- **Bus**: Alilaguna water bus €18 (60-90 min); ACTV Aerobus €10 (25 min to Piazzale Roma); ATVO Express €12 (20 min to Piazzale Roma)
- **Train**: No direct train. Bus to Mestre station then train to Santa Lucia (~€1.50 + €10 bus)
- **Taxi**: Private water taxi €110-130 (30 min, up to 5 pax); shared water taxi ~€30-39 pp

### Treviso (TSF)
- **Bus**: ATVO express bus €14 (60-70 min to Piazzale Roma); Barzi bus €10 (40 min to Tronchetto)
- **Train**: Bus line 6 to Treviso station (€2-3, 15 min) then train to Santa Lucia (€3-5, 30-40 min)
- **Taxi**: ~€80-100 (40-50 min); shared transfer ~€40-60 pp

### Tips
Buy Alilaguna tickets online or at airport kiosk (not ACTV machines). ACTV Aerobus tickets cheaper but stop at Piazzale Roma only. 24h ACTV pass (€25) worth it if using vaporetto same day.

## Day Trips

### Murano
- **What**: Glass-blowing island, Museo del Vetro (€15), authentic Murano glass shopping
- **Transport**: Vaporetto Line 12 from Fondamente Nove, 15 min, €9.50 single or €25 24h pass
- **Best for**: Glass art enthusiasts, souvenir shopping

### Burano
- **What**: Rainbow-coloured fishermen's island, lace-making museum (€5), extremely photogenic
- **Transport**: Vaporetto Line 12 from Fondamente Nove (45 min) or from Murano (30 min)
- **Best for**: Photography, lace shopping

### Torcello
- **What**: Near-deserted island with 12th-century Byzantine cathedral (€5), peaceful atmosphere
- **Transport**: Vaporetto Line 12 from Burano (5 min). Best as third island after Murano + Burano.
- **Best for**: History, Byzantine art, escaping crowds

### Padua
- **What**: Giotto's Scrovegni Chapel frescoes (UNESCO), world's oldest botanical garden, Sant'Antonio Basilica
- **Transport**: Regional train from Santa Lucia, 25-35 min, €4-9.50 each way, every 15-30 min
- **Best for**: Art lovers (Giotto), history buffs

### Verona
- **What**: Roman Arena (opera venue), medieval centre, Juliet's Balcony, great wine
- **Transport**: Regional/high-speed train from Santa Lucia, 1-1.5 hr, €8-15 each way, every 30-60 min
- **Best for**: Romance, Roman architecture, opera lovers
